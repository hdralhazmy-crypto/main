FIFA World Cup 2034 — Overview

A summarized look at Saudi Arabia’s hosting of the 2034 World Cup

Compiled from official and media sources

Basics — Who’s Hosting? Where?

• Host country: Saudi Arabia.
• Tournament includes 48 teams across several cities and stadiums.

Key Details

  • Saudi Arabia won the hosting rights as the only official bid.
  • Expected stadiums: 15 across 5 major cities (Riyadh, Jeddah, Khobar, Abha, NEOM).
  • King Salman Stadium is expected to host the opening match or the final.

Hosting Objectives

Strengthening sports and tourism infrastructure, aligned with Saudi Vision 2030 to become a global destination.

Controversies & Challenges

  • Human rights and labor concerns raised by some international organizations.
  • Summer climate may require adjusting the traditional World Cup schedule.
